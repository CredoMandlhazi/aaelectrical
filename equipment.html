Company-Owned Fleet

Equipment & Plant

AA Electrical maintains a comprehensive fleet of company-owned equipment and plant to ensure operational independence on all project sites. Our investment in owned equipment means no delays waiting for external plant hire — we mobilise quickly and work efficiently.

01
Aircraft — Piper 200-28R

Company aircraft enabling rapid site mobilisation and aerial survey capability across our multi-province service area.

02
UD290 — 16 Ton Crane Truck

Heavy-lift crane truck for erecting street lighting poles, transformer installations, and large cable reel handling on major projects.

03
Cabstar — 4 Ton Crane Truck

Versatile medium crane truck for distribution board installations, cable drum handling, and precision lifts in commercial and residential sites.

04
JCB 4x4 TLB

Four-wheel drive Tractor Loader Backhoe for cable trenching, underground ducting installation, and site preparation in all terrain conditions.

05
Vipac Vibro Compactors

Vibratory plate compactors for reinstatement of trenched surfaces after underground cable and ducting installations.

06
Wacker Rammers

Pneumatic tamping rammers for compaction in confined trenches and restricted areas during underground cable and conduit installation.

07
Cable Car

Specialised cable transport system for safe overhead cable drum handling and stringing on high-voltage and street lighting overhead line installations.

08
Cable Rollers & Reels

Full inventory of cable rollers, cable stands, and drum reels for controlled cable installation in underground ducting systems and overhead reticulation routes.
