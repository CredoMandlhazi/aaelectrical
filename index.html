Our Story
1987
Year Founded

Three Decades of
Electrical Expertise

Since 1987, AA Electrical has been at the forefront of South Africa's electrical infrastructure landscape. From our base in Pretoria, we have grown into a trusted force powering residential estates, commercial centres, factories, and public infrastructure across five provinces.

Our approach is defined by hands-on director involvement and highly skilled tradesmen. Frikkie and Duane van Zyl are personally invested in every project — ensuring quality, compliance, and on-time delivery.

Full-Scope Project Management

From planning and procurement to installation and commissioning — managed by experienced, dedicated project managers.

Director-Led Quality Control

Frikkie and Duane van Zyl maintain active oversight on all projects, ensuring our standards are met from tender to handover.

Health, Safety & Compliance

A dedicated Health & Safety Officer ensures full compliance with all South African electrical safety regulations on every site.
