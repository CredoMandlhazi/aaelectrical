About AA Electrical

A legacy of skilled craftsmanship and professional electrical contracting across South Africa since 1987.

Est. 1987

Built on Craftsmanship,
Led by Experience

AA Electrical was founded in 1987 by experienced electrical professionals who understood that the industry demanded more than technical competence — it demanded integrity, reliability, and hands-on leadership.

Over nearly four decades, the company has grown from a regional contractor to a multi-provincial force, delivering electrical infrastructure for some of South Africa's most significant residential, commercial, and industrial developments.

Directors Frikkie and Duane van Zyl lead from the front. Their direct involvement in project oversight, procurement, and quality control is the foundation of AA Electrical's reputation for delivering projects on time, to specification, and within budget.
